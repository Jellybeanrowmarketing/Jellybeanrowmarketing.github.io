What We Build

Email Marketing & Lead Generation
That Runs Itself.

From the first opt-in to the final purchase — Jellybean Row Marketing, Newfoundland’s #1 email agency, engineers every step of the customer journey for businesses in St. John’s, across Canada, and the United States.

01
📧
Email Automation
Welcome · Nurture · Re-engagement

Set-and-forget email sequences that work around the clock. From the first welcome email to a 12-touch nurture sequence, every message is timed, personalized, and engineered to move prospects closer to a purchase.

  • Welcome sequence (5–7 emails) for all new subscribers
  • Nurture sequences tailored to lead source and behaviour
  • Abandoned cart and browse-abandonment recovery
  • Re-engagement campaigns for cold or inactive lists
  • Post-purchase upsell and cross-sell sequences
  • Platform setup: Klaviyo, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign
02
🎯
Lead Capture
Forms · Lead Magnets · Landing Pages

The best email sequence in the world is useless without leads going in. We build the front end of your funnel — the lead magnets, opt-in forms, and landing pages that turn strangers into subscribers at scale.

  • Lead magnet strategy and copywriting
  • High-converting opt-in landing page design
  • Embedded and popup form optimization
  • A/B testing of headlines, CTAs, and form fields
  • Integration with your CRM and email platform
  • CASL & CAN-SPAM compliance setup
03
📈
Nurture Funnels
Segmentation · Personalization · Conversion

Not all leads are ready to buy right now. We build multi-touch nurture systems that keep your brand top of mind, educate prospects, and deliver the right message at exactly the right moment in their journey.

  • Behavioural segmentation and tagging logic
  • Multi-sequence funnel architecture
  • Dynamic content personalization
  • Sales-ready lead scoring and handoff
  • SMS and email combined sequences
  • Monthly funnel performance reporting
04
💰
Campaign Management
Newsletters · Promos · Broadcasts

Beyond automation, your list needs regular broadcasts that keep subscribers engaged and spending. We plan, write, design, and send your email campaigns so your audience never goes cold.

  • Monthly email campaign calendar
  • Promotional and sales email copywriting
  • Newsletter strategy and production
  • List hygiene and deliverability management
  • Subject line and send-time A/B testing
  • Click-through and revenue attribution reporting
